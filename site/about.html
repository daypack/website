a little bit about me

I work on the web, well more like behind it. I develop things for the online world from websites to online applications.

I am passionate about the Web and have a decade of experience in the industry. My skills have been forged by working in variety of web based companies that have operated in various fields of the web industry from the rolling development life cycles of online application development to the go-go pace of the start-up world and the corporate world of a large successful ecommerce company.

Having mainly worked with front-end technologies I am strongest in the front-end development environment but have developed a healthy understanding and fare ability in the back-end environment. This gives me the advantage of knowing how to bring both environments together smoothly to complete a project.
