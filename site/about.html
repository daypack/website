a little bit about me

I work on the web, well more like behind it. I develop things for the online world from websites to online applications.

I am passionate about the web and have over a decade of experience in the industry. My skills have been forged by working in a variety of web based companies that have operated in various fields of the web industry from the rolling development life cycles of online application development to the go-go pace of the start-up world and the corporate world of a large successful ecommerce company.

My experience working in a variety of online environments has given me a deep understanding of both the design and development aspects of the web, and the know-how to design practical application interfaces that not only complete project goals but are also pleasant and satisfying to use.
